# **App Name**: BioScript Launchpad

## Core Features:

- Homepage Section: Displays information about BioScript.
- About Us Section: Presents details about the company, such as its mission and history.
- Advantages Section: Lists the main benefits of using BioScript.
- Our Team Section: Features an editable section for team members.
- Partners Section: Displays partner logos.
- AI Chatbot Support: Integrates a GEMINI chatbot to provide instant information about BioScript.
- Privacy Policy Link: Includes a link to the company's privacy policy in the footer.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: #00A1D7 for a modern and clean aesthetic.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) to maintain a clean and airy feel.
- Accent color: A slightly darker shade of blue (#0077B3) to provide contrast without being overwhelming.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, to give a modern, objective feel.
- Fully responsive layout to ensure optimal viewing experience across all devices.
- Use a consistent style of simple, clear icons throughout the site to aid comprehension and enhance visual appeal.
- Subtle, unobtrusive animations to add a touch of interactivity without distracting from the content.
